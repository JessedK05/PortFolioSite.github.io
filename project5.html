Fight Club Game:

A classmate of mine and me decided to make a game as group. Considering both our friends didn't really show that much interest we decided to work together. We were brainstorming on what we wanted, we wanted to make a 2D game with streetfighter like and mechanics, looks and controls. Not wanting it to be a Streetfighter rip off we asked around some designers on our school to see if anyone was interested. We got 3 designers onboard and we presented the idea. They liked the idea and wanted to design streetfighter game but with the characters and setting based of the movie Fight Club. Because i didn't know the movie I first watched the movie. And we started the project after I approved with the plan

Mechanics

-Character selection

-Map selection

-Background music for the menus and fighting scene

-Healthbar

-Punching attack

-Ranged Crouch attack

-Jump and crouch to dodge

-Win screen depended on who has won

Thoughts

I'm very proud of this project, not only because it turned out very well in looks and it works pretty good. Now I also know how it's like to work with designers and co-developers to work on the same project, which was also very fun and educational to do.
