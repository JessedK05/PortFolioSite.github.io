Race Game

Because one of my biggest passions is carracing, I decided to make a Racing game like TrackMania based around setting the fastest time around a track. This would learn me more about triggers and physics. This game went pretty good everything worked very well and the car handled well and was fun to drive with. Especially since it was very easy to drift with it. There were no bugs in the game but i could've improved more on the graphics.
