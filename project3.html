2D Fighting Game

For another schoolproject we had to focus our project behind the feedback we got from fellow students. I wanted to do something I've never done before and that was to include an AI that will fight you. It was inspired by games as Brawlhalla and Supersmash when it came to the premise and health system of the game. When we did the feedback round I saw that I had a lot more bugs than I thought I had. For example jumping on the the enemy made you fly faster than the speed of sound and my melee attack didn't work. After some improving I managed to fix the issue and made it a finished game. It was a major learning curve in how feedback can effect the output of a game.

P.S i sadly do not have a github for this project because my project had a lot of corrupted files. I sincerely apologize.
