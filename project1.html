Game Jam:

Me and my friend did a small game jam in which we would make a small tower defense game in just 2,5 days. I'm especially proud of this one because of how far we came in this short time and this could be an actual product we could bring out if we'd actually finish and refine it. This project also really motivated me to work on a project, because we were a team and I defenitely did not want to let my team down.
